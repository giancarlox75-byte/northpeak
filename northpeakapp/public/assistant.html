ELEV. 1900 — QUESTIONS

Common questions.

Will it make things up?

No. The assistant only answers from your approved website content. When it doesn't know, it says so and offers to connect the visitor with your team — which protects your reputation.

Is my data safe?

Each client's content is kept separate and private. We use providers with strong data terms and document exactly how data is handled. See our Privacy Policy.

Do I have to change my website?

No rebuild required. It's a single line of code that drops onto your existing site, whatever it's built with. We can install it for you.

What happens when it can't help?

It gracefully hands off — collecting the visitor's details so your team can follow up, instead of leaving them at a dead end.

How fast can it go live?

Usually within two weeks: we survey your needs, train it on your site, show you a working version, then install it.

ABOUT

We map the terrain before we build the route.

Northpeak Solutions is an AI integration studio. We help businesses put AI to work where it actually saves time — not as a science project, but as a tool that earns its keep.

ELEV. 0300 — WHY WE EXIST

Most businesses don't need more software. They need their time back.

Every team has work that repeats: the same questions answered a hundred times, the same documents read by hand, the same forms triaged every morning. It's expensive, it's draining, and most of it can be handled by AI that's built carefully and pointed at the right problem.

The catch is that generic AI tools rarely fit a specific business. They don't know your policies, your voice, or your edge cases — and a confidently wrong answer costs more than no answer at all. That's the gap Northpeak exists to close: AI that's surveyed to your terrain, integrated into what you already use, and maintained so it stays accurate.

ELEV. 0820 — HOW WE WORK

Principles we don't compromise on.

01 · PROOF FIRST

A working pilot before you commit

We build on your real data and show it working within about two weeks. You judge the value before scope or cost grows.

02 · TRUST BY DESIGN

Guardrails, not guesswork

Every system we ship knows when to say ‘I don’t know’ and hand off to a human. Confidently wrong is worse than honest.

03 · YOUR DATA, RESPECTED

Clear handling, no surprises

We use providers with strong data terms, document where data goes, and keep each client's knowledge base separate and private.

04 · NO RIP-AND-REPLACE

We meet your stack where it is

Our work drops into the website and tools you already run. You don't rebuild anything to work with us.

ELEV. 1400 — THE STUDIO

A focused studio, built for hands-on delivery.

Northpeak is a small, founder-led studio — deliberately. It means the person who surveys your business is the person who builds and maintains your solution, with no handoffs to a junior team and no bloated overhead passed on to you. We take on a limited number of engagements at a time so each one gets real attention.
